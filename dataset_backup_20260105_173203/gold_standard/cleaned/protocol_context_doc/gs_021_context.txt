PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Kyber - KEM Hook (UniswapV4/PancakeSwap)

PROTOCOL TYPE: DEX Hook / Signed Quote System

OVERVIEW:
The Kyber Exclusive Market (KEM) Hook integrates with Uniswap V4 and PancakeSwap Infinity to provide signed quote functionality. Market makers sign off-chain quotes that users can execute on-chain, enabling guaranteed pricing with MEV protection and better execution for traders.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Market makers (quoters) provide signed quotes for token swaps
- Users submit swaps with attached signatures that authorize specific trade parameters
- The hook validates signatures before allowing swaps to proceed
- Quotes include price limits, expiry times, and nonce for replay protection
- Enables professional market making with off-chain quote generation
