PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Hybra Finance - GaugeCL

PROTOCOL TYPE: Concentrated Liquidity Gauge / Fee Collection

OVERVIEW:
GaugeCL is a gauge contract for concentrated liquidity pools that handles both reward distribution and trading fee collection. It collects fees from the underlying pool and routes them to the appropriate recipients including bribe contracts for voter incentives.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Collects trading fees generated by the underlying concentrated liquidity pool
- Routes collected fees to internal and external bribe contracts
- Bribe contracts distribute fees to veNFT voters who supported the pool
- Manages both staking rewards and trading fee distribution
- Fees are collected periodically and forwarded to incentivize governance participation
