PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Velodrome - V2Locker (Pool Launcher)

PROTOCOL TYPE: LP Token Locking / Liquidity Mining

OVERVIEW:
V2Locker is part of Velodrome's pool launcher system, allowing users to lock LP tokens for a specified duration. While locked, users can claim trading fees generated by their liquidity position. The locker manages the lifecycle of locked LP positions.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Users deposit Velodrome V2 LP tokens into a locker contract
- LP tokens are locked until a specified unlock time
- While locked, users can claim their share of trading fees from the pool
- After the lock period, users can unlock and withdraw their LP tokens
- The locker contract holds LP tokens and receives fee distributions
