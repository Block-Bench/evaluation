PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Hybra Finance - CLFactory

PROTOCOL TYPE: AMM Pool Factory / Concentrated Liquidity

OVERVIEW:
CLFactory is a factory contract for creating concentrated liquidity AMM pools. It manages pool deployment, fee configurations, and integrates with a dynamic swap fee module that can adjust trading fees based on market conditions.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Creates and manages concentrated liquidity trading pools
- Supports configurable swap fees per pool via a dynamic fee module
- Governance can set fee parameters including custom fees and fee caps
- Trading fees are collected from swaps and distributed to liquidity providers and protocol
- Tick spacing determines fee tier defaults for different pool types
