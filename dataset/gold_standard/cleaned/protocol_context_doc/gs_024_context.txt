PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Mantle - Staking (mETH)

PROTOCOL TYPE: Liquid Staking / ETH Derivative

OVERVIEW:
The Mantle Staking contract is the core of the mETH liquid staking protocol. Users deposit ETH to receive mETH tokens that represent their share of staked ETH plus accrued rewards. The contract supports permit-based approvals for gasless unstaking requests.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Users stake ETH and receive mETH tokens in return
- mETH represents a share of the total staked ETH and accumulated rewards
- Users can request unstaking which locks their mETH until processed
- Supports EIP-2612 permit signatures for gasless unstake requests
- Exchange rate between mETH and ETH changes based on staking rewards
