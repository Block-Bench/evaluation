PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Mantle - PositionManager (mETH x Aave)

PROTOCOL TYPE: Liquid Staking / Yield Strategy

OVERVIEW:
PositionManager manages the Mantle mETH protocol's position in Aave. It deposits ETH into Aave to earn yield, holds the resulting aWETH tokens, and handles withdrawals back to the LiquidityBuffer. It includes emergency functions for rapid fund recovery.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Receives ETH from LiquidityBuffer and deposits into Aave lending pool
- Holds aWETH representing the protocol's Aave position
- Earns yield from Aave lending for the mETH protocol
- Supports normal withdrawals through the LiquidityBuffer flow
- Has emergency functions for rapid fund recovery in critical situations
