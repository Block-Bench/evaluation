PROTOCOL CONTEXT: Hybra Finance - GaugeV2

PROTOCOL TYPE: Staking Gauge / Reward Distribution

OVERVIEW:
GaugeV2 is a staking contract where users deposit LP tokens to earn protocol rewards. It tracks staked balances, calculates reward accrual over time, and allows users to claim earned rewards. The gauge also includes emergency functionality for situations requiring immediate user access to funds.

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Users stake LP tokens to earn reward tokens over time
- Rewards are distributed proportionally based on stake size and duration
- Protocol notifies the gauge of reward amounts to be distributed over epochs
- Users can deposit, withdraw, and claim rewards at any time
- Emergency mode allows users to exit quickly when activated
